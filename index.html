What we do

By coining the phrase “blessing account”, the platform can track and easily allow for non-profits and social services to gain a wider range of exposure and followers to help support those causes.

Our platform will support various causes that stem from mentoring service veterans, boosting economic development for women owned organizations, providing scholarships, and supporting nonprofit organizations as they create a bigger outreach for their mission.

Blessing Account will revolutionize the way companies increase their philanthropic work and how an individual can measure their own social impact.
